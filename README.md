# zomb-Ai-Pazhamudir-cantai
AI based repo for Vegetable market

## 🌾 Smart Agricultural Market Finder

A simple and elegant tool to help farmers identify the **best markets to sell their produce in Tamil Nadu** based on price, availability, and market details.

---

## 🥬 Supported Produce

The platform supports **28 produce items** including:

- **12 Vegetables**
- **12 Crops / Grains**
- **6 Fruits**

Each item includes:
- 🌐 **English Name**
- 🪔 **Tamil Name**
- 🍎 **Emoji Icon for easy identification**

---

## 🏬 Supported Markets

The system includes **10 major agricultural markets in Tamil Nadu**:

1. Chennai – Koyambedu Market  
2. Coimbatore Market  
3. Madurai Market  
4. Salem Market  
5. Trichy Market  
6. Erode Market  
7. Vellore Market  
8. Thanjavur Market  
9. Tirupur Market  
10. Dindigul Market  

Each market includes:

- 📞 Contact Details  
- 👤 Market In-Charge Name  
- ⏰ Market Timing  
- 📧 Email Address  

---

## 🧠 Smart Form

The platform includes an **intelligent input form** that:

- Allows users to **select yield type**
- Automatically **filters produce dropdown**
- Dynamically updates the **inventory grid**

---

## ⚖️ Quantity & Value Calculation

Farmers can enter produce quantity using:

- **Quintals**
- **Tonnes**
- **Kilograms**

Each produce card shows the **estimated total value** based on entered quantity.

---

## 💰 Market Result Cards

Markets are displayed with the **best price ranked first**.

Each result card includes:

- ⭐ **Best Price Badge**
- 💵 Price Range
- ⏰ Market Timing
- 🏷️ Produce Grade Tag
- 📞 **Call Button**
- 🔍 **View Details Button**

---

## 📇 Contact Modal

Clicking **View Details** opens a modal with:

- 👤 Market In-Charge Name
- 📞 Phone Number
- 📧 Email Address
- ☎️ **Direct Call Button**

---

## 📦 Inventory Grid

The inventory section allows farmers to:

- Tap any produce item to **auto-select it in the form**
- Filter items using **produce type tabs**

---
